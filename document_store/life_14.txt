Can I sign my documents electronically?

Yes. If you complete the application online you can sign electronically.
You can also sign documents electronically if the application is submitted by an agent and they select Email Initiated Signatures. Once the agent selects the electronic signature option, you will receive an email with a link to apply signatures.
Note: Email signature options are available based on state and policy selection type.
