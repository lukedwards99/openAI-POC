What is the annual deadline for contributing to my traditional or Roth IRA?

You can make annual contributions to a traditional or Roth IRA from January 1 through the tax-filing deadline (excluding extensions) for the contribution year. For example, 2023 contributions can be made from January 1, 2024 through April 15, 2024.
