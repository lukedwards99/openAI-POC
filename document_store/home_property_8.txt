Why are you asking for my Social Security number?
Providing your Social Security number (SSN) is optional. However, providing a SSN is the most accurate way to help us identify consumer reporting information related to our applicants.

Your SSN is treated as confidential information in accordance with the State Farm privacy policy. We value our customers and potential customers and take privacy seriously.
